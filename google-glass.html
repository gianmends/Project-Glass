Screen

How the world will appear

According to sources close to Google, the glasses will feature a small LCD or AMOLED screen at the top and in front of the user's eyes. With the use of a camera and GPS, you can locate yourself, as well as select options with the movement of your head.

Camera

Film and photograph anytime

With Google Glass it will be possible to take pictures with up to 5 megapixels and record videos with 720 lines of resolution. The first videos and photos captured with the device have already started to circulate on the network, but so far no one has much technical information.

Sensors

The sensitivity of a simple glasses

Anyone who thinks that to control Google Glass will need a keyboard and mouse, is completely mistaken. The device comes with various types of sensors and built-in microphones. Thus, to give a command, just speak or swipe your finger on the side of the glasses.

Battery and Gadgets

What are the devices that complement Glass

According to Google itself, Glass will come with a battery that has enough autonomy to last a whole day. Only some activities like videoconferencing and long filming will require a little more. Plus it comes with WiFi, Bluetooth, 3G/4G and more.
